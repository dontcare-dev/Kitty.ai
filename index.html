FAQ

Common Questions

How do daily limits work?▾
Each model has its own daily cap. Haiku: 100/day, Sonnet: 50/day, Opus: 30/day. Resets at midnight. Unused requests don't roll over.
Which model should I use?▾
Haiku for quick lookups. Sonnet for coding and analysis. Opus for deep research and complex reasoning.
Is my data private?▾
Conversations are stored in your browser only. No server sync. Input sanitization and XSS protection throughout.
Can I export my conversations?▾
Yes — Settings → Export Conversations downloads your full history as a JSON file.
